üí° Our Recommendation

Option 1: Tournament-Style Testing is the most strategic approach for most budgets.

This data-driven method ensures you invest your full budget into the video that performs best with your actual audience, rather than guessing which one will work.

By testing in stages, you'll identify your "hero" video and maximize ROI while minimizing production costs.
