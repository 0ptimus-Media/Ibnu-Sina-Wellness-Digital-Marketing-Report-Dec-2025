üé• Reels & Stories Content Strategy

üéØ Strategic Framework: 60-30-10 Rule

60% Educational Content - Build trust and authority

30% Engagement Content - Drive interaction and shareability

10% Promotional Content - Direct conversion asks

üì± Reels Content Pillars (Organic + Paid)

Pillar 1: Patient Transformation Stories (60-Second Reels)

Objective: Social proof and emotional connection

Content Format:

  • Hook (3 sec): "After 10 years of medication, this is what happened..."
  • Problem showcase (10 sec): Patient shares their struggle
  • Solution introduction (15 sec): Natural treatment approach
  • Results (20 sec): Before/after, specific improvements
  • CTA (7 sec): "Link in bio for free health assessment"

Production: Patient testimonial in vertical format, text overlays, background music

Frequency: 3-4 per week (organic + boosted)

Pillar 2: Educational "Did You Know" Series (30-45 Seconds)

Objective: Position Ibnu Sina as a health authority and trusted information source

Content Format: Short, educational videos with subtitles and graphics

  • Health benefits of specific treatments or preventative care
  • Wellness tips and seasonal health advice
  • Debunking common health myths
  • Quick wellness hacks and home remedies
  • Importance of regular check-ups and prevention

Production: In-house with minimal editing, featuring doctors or wellness experts

Frequency: 2-3 per week (organic + boosted)

Pillar 3: Promotional Content (15-30 Seconds)

Objective: Drive specific actions - appointments, consultations, or service inquiries

Content Format: Direct CTA with service highlights and limited-time offers

  • New service launches with introductory pricing
  • Seasonal health packages and promotions
  • Referral incentives and loyalty program highlights
  • Special discounts for first-time patients
  • Appointment booking made easy - walk-through

Production: Product shoots, service highlights, and professional testimonials

Frequency: 1-2 per week (primarily paid)
